## Topic

Replication and extension of AXIOM (Heins et al., 2025): an active inference agent
that learns object-centric world models using expanding Bayesian mixture models.

## Connection to Bayesian Inference

- Conjugate Bayesian updates (Normal-Inverse-Wishart, Dirichlet)
- Variational inference (free energy minimization)
- Nonparametric model expansion (stick-breaking / Dirichlet process priors)
- Bayesian Model Reduction for structure pruning
- Active inference: action selection through expected free energy

## Planned Deliverables

- Working replication of AXIOM on the Gameworld 10k benchmark
- Comparison with BBF and DreamerV3 baselines
- Ablation studies (no BMR, no information gain, fixed interaction distance)
- 4–5 page report and 8-minute presentation
